The basic concept is a 1024×1024×512 voxel world with horizontal wrap around, where the voxels are half a meter (1 minecraft voxel = 23 of my voxels).

Art Direction

Procedural pixelart textures for the voxels, dynamically render pixelart bilboards where possible. Limited color palette.

Voxels

The current idea is maybe 8 bits per voxel, 0–31 (32) are air with different light values, 32–39 (8) are different water levels, while 40–255 are block types.

List of blocks:

  • Basalt: somewhat columnar
  • Sandstone: sand-colored, with darker lines
  • Limestone: warm, light, flat color
  • Amphibolite: dark rock with bright speckles
