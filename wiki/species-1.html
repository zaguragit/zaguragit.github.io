Species 1
Diet:herbivorous
Scientific Classification
Domain:Eukaryota
Kingdom:Animalia

Are an intelligent herbivorous hermaphroditic species.

Appearance

Height

Typically between 1.9m & 2.5m

Coloration

They can be any color, and it really depends on their self-actualization more than genetics

Development

Everyone starts out with a desaturated, greyish coloration, and really short, rounded horns. As they grow, they slowly self-actualize, and their traits become more unique. Several characteristics are dependant on their self-perception:
  • Coloration
  • Horn size/shape
  • Height, to a certain extent (tho it's more dependant on genetics)
When they have an existencial crisis, or are emotionally going through a really difficult time, their body often reverts to it's un-actualized state

Reproduction

Their eggs start at around an 8cm radius, and grow to around a 20cm radius, which is made possible by having a hard, but growable membrane
