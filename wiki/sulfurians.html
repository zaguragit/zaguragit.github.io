Sulfurians

Last Updated: Nov 17, 2023

Sulfurians are a highly authoritarian and religious nation. Classed into mages and soldiers, based on the development of each individual. They have an ability to do magic, but it’s considered to be the job of the mages only, and if a soldier engages in magic, it’s considered an abomination and is punishable by exile.

Appearance

Pale, somewhat humid skin, gray eyes with black sclera & thin ears. Mages often look shorter and have colder skin tones, while soldiers are normally taller, with warmer, beige skin.
