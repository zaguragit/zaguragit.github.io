Mission

The computer must be capable to being a decent music player, so it must support at least 1TB of storage. It must be capable of rudimentary multitasking, and have protected virtual memory, to have apps that don't crash the entire system. This discards the possibility of using 8 and 16 bits (unless im wrong and all of that can be achieved with just 16 bits).

I want the design to be as simple & cheap as possible, to increase the device's lifespan, and reduce its production costs.
