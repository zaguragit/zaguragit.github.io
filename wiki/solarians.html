Solarians

Last Updated: Nov 17, 2023

Solarians are considered to be very outgoing and energetic, sometimes with a carefree attitude about life. They value self expression very highly and are attributed a very colorful aesthetic. They are quite individualistic and autonomous, normally living by themselves or in small groups (2 to 4 people) per house.

Appearance

Orange, yellow or golden skin, except belly, chest, sometimes the lower front neck and part of the inner thighs, which are a cream color. Skinnier and slightly taller than nordics on average. Have no hair anywhere on their bodies, except their bushy eyebrows and big eyelashes.
