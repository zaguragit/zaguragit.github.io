Tectonic Plates

Tectonic plates are divided into oceanic and continental. Oceanic plates are those that are covered by water, while continental ones are those that, u guessed it, are continents.

There are 3 different types of interactions between tectonic plates:

  • Divergent Motion: plates move apart from eachother, revealing the magma underneath
  • Convergent Motion: plates move towards eachother, pushing one above and one below
  • Transform Motion: plates grind past eachother

There can be hotspots under tectonic plates, which as the plate moves, can poke holes through it, releasing magma and creating an island
