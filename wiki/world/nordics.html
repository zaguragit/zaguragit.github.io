Nordics

Last Updated: Nov 17, 2023

Nordics live in a cold environment with few resources and don’t like to be wasteful. They are generally very caring and known for their hospitality. They are more collectivist than the solarians, tending to organize themselves as very large families instead of a market economy, and living in households in large groups.

Appearance

Dark red or magenta skin, with short, subtle stripes on the back, sometimes partially covering arms and thighs. Normally aren’t skinny (unless very unhealthy). Have peachfuzz on their entire body.

Expertise

Nordics’ primary good are their mushrooms, as they simply can’t survive in the solarian climate, they’re also known for their fabrics.
