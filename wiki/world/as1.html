AS1
Length (not including tail)8–15cm

The AS1 is a small, highly territorial animal, that primarily feeds off of insects and plants. They can live both in herds and solitarily.

Their bodies are covered in scales, and their appearance varies between regional subspecies. They have 2 tusks coming out of their cheeks, above their mouth, to defend themselves and attack intruders.

Social Behavior

AS1 normally form small herds, which eventually grow and split up into smaller herds again due to a lack of resources to sustain everyone at the same time. Both the new herds abandon their original home, and seek a new place to sustain themselves. If there are enough resources, the herd may grow indefinitely, and never split. Sometimes, if the resources are scarce enough, the AS1 may choose to live solitarily.

Subspecies

Gray AS1

Lives on mountains, has gray coloring, and is skinnier.

Sand AS1

Lives in deserts and savannas, usually in a symbiotic relationship with PM1. Slightly more body fat than the Gray AS1. Their coloring ranges from brown to sand-colored.
