AS1 (unnamed)

Last Updated: Jan 8, 2024
AS1
Height:1.5-2.2m

The AS1 is a small, highly territorial animal, that primarily feeds off of insects and plants. It can live in both in small herds or alone.

Their bodies are covered in scales, and their appearance varies between regional subspecies. They have 2 tusks to defent themselves and attack intruders.

Social Behavior

AS1 form small herds, which eventually grow and split up into smaller herds again. All the new herds abandon their original home, and seek a new place to sustain themselves. Sometimes, due to resource scarcity they choose to live solitarily to not have to worry about sharing resources.
