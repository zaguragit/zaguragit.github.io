I haven't made a name for it yet, and I'm not sure how I will, but here's my conworld. The world is a small terrestrial planet with a single moon. It has 2 main landmasses. These are populated by, along with other creatures, the {people species}, an inteligent anthropomorphic species.

Sky

During The Day

During the day the sky color ranges from gray to blueish gray, depending on the weather. The sun looks larger and brighter than it does on Earth. The shadows are blurrier than on Earth.

At Sunset

The sky color is usually red, the stars aren't visible at this stage yet.

At Night

At night the skies are a warm brownish color, or a really dark gray at high places. The moon is slightly reddish, and the stars are quite visible.

Plants

Plants tipically range from warm green to red, but can also be a blue-ish color in more shady places.

Natural History

Climate

Since the planet is smaller than earth and closer to the sun, it's warmer on the poles, but quite similar to earth in the equator, temperature-wise.
