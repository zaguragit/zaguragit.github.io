Last Updated: Jan 17, 2024
PM1
Height:1-2m

The PM1 is a cactus-like plant, most often found in deserts, and ocasionally in savannas. It's made up of 5 hard sections around a soft nucleous.

It has a symbiotic relationship with the AS1. The PM1 provides shelter, water and nutrients, and in exchange, the AS1 defends it from larger animals like AM1.
