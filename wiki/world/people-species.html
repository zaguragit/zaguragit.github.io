People Species
DietHerbivorous

Are an intelligent herbivorous hermaphroditic species, that populates {world}.

Appearance

Their skin can either be covered with peachfuzz, or be completely hairless, and its color ranges from golden yellow to a burgundy red, depending on the ethnicity. The belly, lower front neck & inner thighs might be a different color to the rest of the body.

When it comes to size, they're a bit slimmer & taller (1.5–2.2m) than humans.

They have long, pointy ears that point back, and, depending on the ethnicity, upwards, downwards, or somewhere in between.

They have horns, but they're really small, and their shape and size varies.

Social Behavior

{People species} live in tribes/flocks/communes. They usually mix all the children in one group, and assign people responsible for taking care of them. In most cultures, the concept of caretaker is the equivalent to our concept of parent, and the biological parents have no special relation to their children.

Members if this species are usually disinterested in power for power's sake, and are highly cooperative. One of their most primary needs is to feel useful to their commune, which is what drives most of their innovation, conflicts and discoveries instead. If an individual can't contribute to their commune in any way at all, they may suffer severe mental health issues.

Reproduction

Mating

{People species} have evolved past hard-wired mating rituals, so partner desirability is dictated by each specific culture, and their own personal preferences.

Eggs

Their eggs start at around an 8cm diameter, and grow to around 20cm, which is made possible by having a slightly flexible membrane, that slowly grows by using up part of the protein stored inside it, while the embryo uses the rest to grow them-selves. They're usually red-colored.

They typically store all of the eggs in a common place, and assign members of their commune the responsibility to care for and protect them, tho it may vary by culture.
