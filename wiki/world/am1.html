AM1
Height:1.5-2.2m

The AM1 is an animal that lives in hot deserts, and sometimes savannas. It's a curly-haired quadruped (somewhat similar to a camel), with an abnormally strong neck and a long, sturdy beak.

They feed mostly on PM1, penetrating the hard shell with their beak, and using their thin long tongue to suck all of the nutrients and water out.

Their peaks can be used for food and for self-defense.
