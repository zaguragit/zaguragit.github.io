AM1
DietHerbivorous
Height1.5–2.2m

The AM1 is a solitary animal that lives in hot deserts, and can rarely be found in savannas. It's a quadruped with long, thin legs, and curly fur (somewhat similar to a camel), with an abnormally thick, strong neck and a long, sturdy beak, that can be used for both food, and self-defense. They have a single hump on the back, that helps them preserve water. Their head has an appearance reminiscent of a skull, and serves to protect them from protector symbiotes in plants when they feed.

Diet

The AM1 are herbivorous, and mostly feed on PM1, penetrating the hard shell with their beak, and using their thin long tongue to suck all of the nutrients and water out.
