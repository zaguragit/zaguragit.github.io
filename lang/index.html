Constraints

Syllables must follow the structure CV(N) (consonant + vowel + n, m).
/t/, /k/, /s/ are used at the start of a word, unless followed by /a/, while /d/, /g/, /z/ are used elsewhere.
The combinations ji/ij & wu/uw are not allowed
