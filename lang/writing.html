Capitallization

  • First letter in sentense
  • First letter of proper or foreign words

Numbers

Numbers (which are base 6), are written in arabic form. Do whatever you want, but "," won't be part of a number

Punctuation

Ilin Kil uses the same punctuation as most european languages
