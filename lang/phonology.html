Constraints

  • The first syllable is always the stressed one (obviously, if ur singing, do whatever u want)
  • Syllables must follow the structure:
    • V(n, m, l) only at the start of the word
    • CV(n, m, l)
  • The combinations /ij/ & /nm/ are not allowed
