Ilin Kil's Syntax

main page: Ilin Kil

Ilin Kil has an SVO (subject, verb, object) structure. Adjectives follow nouns, adverbs & predicatives follow verbs. There's no distinction between adjectives, nouns & pronouns: the first one is treated as a noun, and the rest as modifiers. The one exception are numerals, which precede their noun

Proper Names & Foreign Words

Both proper names & foreign words must be used as a modifier/adjective after a noun, and are always capitalized
