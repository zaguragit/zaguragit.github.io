Woke Insults

Insults that only hurt their target

The current state of insults in most languages & cultures is… suboptimal. Most are either ableist, queerphobic or misogynistic, or foster sex-negativity. The ok ones are mostly about poop, which is fine, but I feel like we deserve a more varied swearword vocabulary. This is a page where I'll collect the best non-problematic insults I find

Insult (or swear-word) Requirements

  • No problematic origin (etymology)
  • No problematic meaning now
